Our Story

Founded in 2025 after our CEO struggled to find affordable career guidance, CareerConnect was born to ensure no student faces career uncertainty alone.

What began as a personal challenge transformed into a nationwide mission to make expert guidance accessible, affordable, and effective for every learner.

Mission: To democratize career counselling through technology and human expertise.

Vision: A world where every student discovers their potential.
